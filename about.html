About Me

Angela's love of nature started in her childhood where she spent time exploring the countryside and coast around her home in Scarborough and she has honed her craft over several years of careful observation and dedication to painting. She gathers her inspiration from the smallest creature to the biggest landscape.
Angela uses locally sourced offcuts of wood as the base for many of her paintings, adding an additional texture to her work, utilised to maximum effect. She also paints on canvas and is skilled in the use of mixed media to represent her beautiful animals and birds in their full glory.
